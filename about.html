About

Matchplay, carefully recorded.

We are building the most complete and reliable archive of match play in professional golf. Our focus is on preserving every result, presenting the data clearly, and capturing the head-to-head contests that define the format.

The archive covers the Ryder Cup, Presidents Cup, WGC Match Play, and Seve Trophy, and is continually reviewed and refined for accuracy. If you identify an error or can help verify a result, we welcome your input. The PGA Championship (Matchplay Era), Summer Olympics, British PGA Matchplay Championship and the World Match Play Championship data is a work in progress!

This project is independent and data-driven. Our approach prioritises transparent methodology, trusted sources, and an experience designed to work seamlessly across devices.
