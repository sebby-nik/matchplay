About Us

Matchplay, documented with care.

We are building the most complete archive of singles match play in elite golf. Our goal is to preserve every result, make the data easy to explore, and celebrate the moments that define head-to-head competition.

The database covers the Ryder Cup, Presidents Cup, and WGC Match Play, and is continually refined for accuracy. If you spot an error or have a source to validate a result, we would love to hear from you.

This project is independent and data-driven. We prioritize clear methodology, transparent sourcing, and usability on every device.
